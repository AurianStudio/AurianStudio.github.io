Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Aurian Client collects, how it is used, and your rights. We are committed to transparency — this reflects exactly what data we touch and why.

1. What We Collect

Hardware Identifier (HWID)

Aurian collects your device's hardware identifier for authentication and abuse prevention. It is tied to your machine, not your personal identity.

Minecraft Username

Your Minecraft username may be logged on launch to associate your session with your HWID for support purposes.

Client Version & Launch Data

We collect the version of Aurian you're running and basic launch info (Minecraft version, launcher type) to ensure compatibility.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect your IP address for tracking purposes.
  • We do not collect gameplay data, chat messages, or in-game actions.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not use cookies or browser tracking of any kind.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication — ensuring only legitimate users can run the Client.
  • Abuse prevention — banning HWIDs associated with unauthorized use.
  • Version tracking — understanding which builds are in active use.
  • Support — helping diagnose issues when you reach out to us.

4. Data Storage & Security

Collected data is stored securely with access restricted to AurianStudio team members on a need-to-know basis. We do not store data in plaintext.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you, request deletion of your data (subject to abuse prevention exceptions), and ask questions about data handling. Contact us via Discord.

6. Children's Privacy

Aurian is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach out on Discord via the link on the home page.
