[ 04 ] Questions

Stuff people ask.

×

Is it actually free?

Yes, completely. Aurian is free and will stay free. We may add optional supporter perks in the future but the core client will always be free.

×

Does it affect my FPS?

Aurian is built to be lightweight. Most users see no FPS drop — in many cases performance actually improves compared to a vanilla setup.

×

What versions does it work on?

1.8.9 currently. It works on Lunar Client, Forge, and Vanilla. Other versions are not supported yet but are on the roadmap.

×

What does the client send back?

Your HWID is logged for authentication and abuse prevention. No personal data, no gameplay recording. Read our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.

×

Is Aurian open source?

Not currently. The client is closed source. We may open parts of it in the future as the project matures.

×

Where can I get support?

Join the Aurian Discord server. The community and dev team are active there — it's the fastest way to get help or report a bug.
